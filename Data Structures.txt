Zones: (Zone_Array is global variable for the zone array)
[ID, Location, Size, Control]
	:ID (Integer)
	:Location ([Float, Float])
	:Size (Float)
	:Control (Int) 0 = BLUFOR, 1 = OPFOR, 2 = IND, 3 = CIV, 10 = None
	
Zone Marker Naming Conventions:
CenterZoneX --> Text at center of zone.  X is zone ID number.
ZoneX --> Shaded zone marker.  X is zone ID number.
